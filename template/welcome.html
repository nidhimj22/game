Cyber Security Game

Welcome

The study involves a live interaction between you and other randomly matched individual from a pool of individuals available online. Furthermore, anonymity will be preserved between you and your partner all along the experiment.
The total duration of this study is approximately 30 minutes. Please close this page if you are not able or willing to spend 30 minutes on this study. Payoffs are always displayed in points, and your goal is to obtain as many points as possible.
Through your participation, you can earn base payment of Rs. 20. Note that you will only be paid if both you and your partner have fully completed the study. Because you will be interacting with another individual, we ask that you pay attention to the task and refrain from interruptions during this study.
